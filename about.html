About AI Tools

A learning-first catalog of AI tools curated for students, researchers & educators.

Mission

Help learners quickly find trustworthy AI tools with clear categories, short descriptions, and real examples — so they can learn, prototype and build responsibly.

What we provide

  • Curated categories & example tools
  • Student-contributed reviews & usage notes
  • Submission form for new tools (reviewed before listing)
